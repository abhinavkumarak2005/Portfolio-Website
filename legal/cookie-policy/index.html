Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on websites built with BentoVibe, a Framer portfolio template.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide insights into how the site is used.

2. How BentoVibe Uses Cookies

BentoVibe itself does not set or require cookies by default.

However, websites created using this template may use cookies for the following purposes, depending on how the site owner configures the site:

  • Essential cookies Necessary for basic site functionality

  • Analytics cookies To understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., page views, traffic sources)

  • Preference cookies To remember user settings such as language or theme

  • Third-party cookies Set by embedded services like videos, fonts, or analytics tools

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some content or features on websites using BentoVibe may rely on third-party services (such as analytics providers or embedded media). These services may place cookies on your device in accordance with their own cookie and privacy policies.

BentoVibe does not control or manage these cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored

  • Block cookies entirely

  • Delete cookies automatically when closing the browser

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some websites.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or site functionality. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy or how cookies are used on a website built with BentoVibe, please contact the site owner directly.
